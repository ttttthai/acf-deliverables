This implementation plan translates the ABC Corporate Strategy into year-by-year milestones, initiatives, KPI targets, investment requirements, and risk factors. The plan covers five years (FY2026-FY2030) across three strategic stages: STABILIZE & FIX (Year 1-2), GROW & EXPAND (Year 2-4), and LEAD & INNOVATE (Year 4-5).
