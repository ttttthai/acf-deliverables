Overview: PISMO is a cloud-native, API-first platform specializing in card issuing, core banking, digital wallets, and digital lending. Acquired by Visa in 2024 for USD 1B, remaining network-agnostic.

Capabilities (from reference document):

Cards Issuing Key Features:

  • Network agnostic: Certified on Visa, Mastercard, and local rails
  • Complete card offering: Single/dual message authorization for debit, prepaid, credit, combo cards
  • Virtual cards with dynamic CVV and flexible expiration
  • Flex Controls: Customizable spending rules per customer/program
  • Real-time event data streaming
  • Dispute management, CRM integration
  • End-to-end lifecycle management (billing, refinancing, revolving credit)
  • Embossing integration with major global partners

Core Banking Key Features:

  • Instant account opening via API
  • Products Factory: Savings, deposits, interest-bearing, overdrafts
  • 100% real-time processing (zero blocking batches)
  • Multi-account structures, multi-currency
  • Financial accounting controls at account level
  • Parent-child relationships, dormancy rules, transaction controls

Digital Lending (NEW module):

  • Complete lifecycle: Booking to disbursement to repayment to settlement
  • Simulation engine for loan scenarios
  • Amortized reducing or add-on interest calculation
  • Flexible repayment: Weekly, fortnightly, monthly, quarterly
  • Bullet/balloon repayment, moratorium/holiday periods
  • Penalty calculation and partial prepayments

Partnership Ecosystem:

  • eKYC: Onfido, Jumio, ShuftiPro, Signzy
  • Fraud: ThetaRay, Feedzai, ComplyAdvantage
  • Collections: Creditas, Credgenics
  • Credit Decisioning: Equifax, Experian, TransUnion, LendingMetrics

4. eKYC & Identity Verification

4.1 Vietnam eKYC Ecosystem

Vietnam's eKYC landscape is shaped by the national CCCD (Citizen ID Card) with embedded NFC chip and the VNeID digital identity platform managed by the Ministry of Public Security.

Regulatory Framework:

  • SBV Circular 16/2020/TT-NHNN: Governs eKYC for account opening
  • Personal Data Protection Decree (Decree 13<span style="position:relative;display:inline-block;cursor:help;margin-left:3px" title="State Bank of Vietnam (sbv.gov.vn)"Decree 13/2023iGovernment "Decree 13/2023/ND-CP" — Personal Data Protection/ND-CP" — Personal Data Protection"UBank and FE Credit Proposal_with pricing_20251128.pdf" (OtherData/WeBank)"Introduction to PISMO.pdf" (OtherData)">i/2023/ND-CP): Data handling requirements
  • SBV allows video-based eKYC and chip-based authentication for finance companies
  • Target: 50% of adult population with personal digital signature by 2025; 70% by 2030
