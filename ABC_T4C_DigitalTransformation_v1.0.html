Executive Summary

ABC's digital maturity is at the bottom of the Vietnam consumer finance market. The company scores 1/5 on digital customer experience, has no mobile app, no BNPL product, manual loan origination processes, eKYC drop-off rates estimated above 25%, and less than 1% of originations through digital channels. Every competitor -- from market leader FE Credit (FE ONLINE 2.0 with 14M users) to the smallest player VietCredit (digital-first model achieving 89.4% growth) -- has surpassed ABC in digital capability.

This roadmap defines a 4-phase digital transformation over 60 months, progressing from emergency fixes to market leadership. The transformation is not optional -- it is existential. Without a mobile app, ABC cannot acquire digital-native customers. Without AI scoring, ABC cannot manage credit risk. Without cybersecurity, ABC cannot pass regulatory inspection.

Total Digital Transformation Investment: USD 18-28MESTIMATE over 5 years (included within Technology Strategy envelope)

Theme: "Stop the Bleeding -- Fix What's Broken"
