EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Introduction

AEON Consumer Finance Vietnam ("ACF", formerly PTF — Post and Telecommunication Finance Company) is a consumer finance company wholly owned by AEON Financial Service Co., Ltd. (AFS Japan). Following AFS's acquisition in early 2025 and subsequent merger with ACSTV (AEON Credit Service Technology Vietnam, AEON Group's installment loan entity), ACF operates under a consumer finance company license issued by the State Bank of Vietnam (SBV).

ACF's product portfolio comprises cash loans (Standard, Flexible, Emergency, Loyalty) and installment loans (Electronics, Furniture, Healthcare, Education, Travel). The company maintains a technology stack centred on Temenos T24 R25 (core banking), LOOMSiACF IT Division "LOS_Gantt-chart_General.xlsx" Package 11 Dec 2025 (Loan Origination & Management System, migrated from Newgen), and NexustiiACF IT Division — Integration Architecture, Package 13 Feb 2026 4.0 (customer-facing platform). Sales are primarily generated through Telesales (~67%i of disbursement) and NSN Agent Network (~33%), with the AEON Retail channel contributing modest volumes. Digital self-origination remains below 1%.

IKIGAI Corporation was engaged to conduct an Operational Due Diligence across three focus areas: (I) Customer Acquisition, (II) Underwriting, and (III) Loan Management. The scope covers end-to-end process mapping, system dependency analysis, manual vs. automated step identification, and key operational risk assessment. The review period covers data as of January 2026.
