Executive Summary

This document benchmarks best practices across 10 critical dimensions of consumer finance operations. For each dimension, we identify (1) the Vietnam best-in-class performer, (2) the global gold standard, (3) the target benchmark, (4) ABC's current gap, and (5) specific copy recommendations. ABC currently trails peers on 8 of 10 dimensions and is critically behind on digital/technology, BNPL, and data analytics. However, ABC's untapped ABC ecosystem advantage provides a springboard to close gaps rapidly if execution is prioritized.

It integrates cash loans, credit card applications, digital banking, transaction lookup, and loan management into a single app. The platform supports 14M+ customers and includes an AI chatbot handling 130,000+ queries monthly. Dimension 4: Ecosystem Integration and Cross-Sell

Vietnam Best: HD Saison / HDBank

HD Saison's ecosystem model is the most sophisticated in Vietnam. The HDBank-HD Saison-VietJet triangle connects 14.6 million combined customers (2024) through the SkyJoy loyalty platform with 14+ million members. Key elements: cross-platform account opening between HDBank and HD Saison, co-branded credit cards, "Fly now, Pay later" airplane ticket financing through VietJet, travel insurance cross-sell, combined 50,000+ physical touch points, and customer graduation pathway (HD Saison low-income customers graduate to HDBank as income grows).

Financial impact: HDBank achieved 80%IKIGAI PROJECTION digital customer acquisition, 94%IKIGAI PROJECTION digital transaction share, and 28.8% total operating income growth in FY2024.

Global Best: Ant Group / Alipay Ecosystem (China)

Ant Group's ecosystem connects 1+ billion users across payments (Alipay), lending (Ant Credit Pay, Huabei), wealth management (Yu'e Bao), insurance (Ant Insurance), and credit scoring (Zhima Credit). The ecosystem creates a closed loop: merchants accept Alipay, consumers spend on Alipay, spending data feeds credit scoring, credit enables more spending. Conversion from payment users to credit users exceeds 40%.

Gold Standard

World-class ecosystem integration requires: unified customer identity across all group entities, single loyalty/rewards currency redeemable everywhere, automated cross-sell triggers based on lifecycle events, shared data platform for 360-degree customer view, seamless product switching (e.g., installment to revolving to credit card), customer graduation pathways between entities, and ecosystem-exclusive benefits that increase switching costs.

  • Plan exists to expand WAON across all group companies by 2026

  2. Short-term: Implement unified customer identity linking WAON, ACS, and PTF customer records. Deploy CRM system (EY recommendation AP02) for 360-degree customer view and lead management.

FE Credit's Pega-based collection system represents the most advanced debt management platform among Vietnamese FinCos. Nubank uses AI-driven collection strategies that segment delinquent borrowers by probability of payment, optimal contact channel, best time to contact, and personalized repayment plan options. The system automatically escalates through channels: push notification, in-app message, SMS, email, phone call, and finally legal action. ML models predict which borrowers will self-cure, allowing resources to focus on accounts that benefit most from intervention. Collection rates are 20-30%ESTIMATE higher than rule-based approaches.

Gold Standard

Modern collections should include: AI-powered segmentation of delinquent accounts, automated multi-channel outreach (app, SMS, email, call, WhatsApp), predictive models for self-cure probability, dynamic repayment plan generation, real-time agent dashboards with next-best-action recommendations, integrated legal and agency management, and automated regulatory compliance (contact frequency, timing, language).

ABC's collection system uses Debt Collection 4.0 and NewGen for default management. Per the EY application heatmap, collection capabilities include: collect payment, monitor delinquency, default management, manage legal, manage recovery, mandate/auto-debit, and agency management. However, these processes involve significant manual intervention, and the platform lacks AI-powered segmentation or predictive capabilities.

Copy Recommendations

  2. Short-term: Implement automated multi-channel outreach (SMS, push notification, Zalo) for early-stage delinquency (DPD 1-30).
  3. Medium-term: Deploy ML-based collection scoring to predict self-cure probability and optimize contact strategies. Integrate with new CRM system for unified customer communication history.
  4. Long-term: Build AI-powered collection engine with dynamic repayment plan generation and automated escalation pathways.

Dimension 6: Digital Onboarding and eKYC

Vietnam Best: MCredit / MB Bank

MCredit's digital onboarding achieves loan approval in under 2 minutes through API-based integration with MB Bank's systems. The process includes: digital identity verification, automated CIC bureau check, real-time credit scoring, instant approval/decline decision, and electronic contract signing. Global Best: Nubank (Brazil)

Nubank's onboarding requires only a smartphone and national ID. The process completes in under 5 minutes using: AI-powered document scanning and OCR, liveness detection (anti-spoofing), real-time background verification, instant credit limit assignment, and immediate card activation (virtual card available instantly, physical card delivered within days). Approval rates exceed 50%ESTIMATE of applications with NPL rates remaining below industry average.

Gold Standard

Best-in-class digital onboarding should achieve: sub-5-minute end-to-end completion, biometric verification (face + fingerprint), OCR-based document capture (national ID, proof of income), real-time bureau check and scoring, instant approval for 70%ESTIMATE+ of applications, electronic contract with digital signature, immediate disbursement (virtual card or bank transfer), and less than 10%ESTIMATE eKYC drop-off rate.

ABC Current Gap: SIGNIFICANT (Score: 2/5ESTIMATE)

ABC is known to have significant eKYC drop-off issues. The current stack includes FPT eKYC, Blacklist Web, Trusting Social, and H2H CIC integration. However, the EY assessment notes: manual processes in the Newgen LOS lead to errors, and the overall digital onboarding experience results in material customer abandonment. The system lacks a customer-facing app, forcing reliance on branch-based and partner-based origination.

Copy Recommendations

  1. Immediate: Audit and fix eKYC drop-off points. Identify specific steps causing abandonment (document upload quality, liveness detection failures, CIC timeout errors).
  2. Short-term: Enhance LOS integration with eKYC, e-Sign, and e-Contract systems for seamless loan applications (EY recommendation AP03). Target sub-10-minute origination.
  3. Medium-term: Implement customer-facing mobile onboarding with biometric verification, real-time credit scoring, and instant approval. Target 2-minute decision for pre-qualified WAON members.
  4. Long-term: Achieve MCredit-level speed (2-minute approval) for digital-first origination channel.
