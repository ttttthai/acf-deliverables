Executive Summary

This report identifies technology capability gaps across five dimensions: (1) current business needs, (2) credit card launch readiness, (3) BNPL launch readiness, (4) 2-wheeler financing readiness, (5) industry best practice, and (6) regulatory requirements. Each gap is assessed for severity and remediation priority, with investment estimates where quantifiable.

ABC faces 38 distinct technology capability gaps across these dimensions. The most critical gaps are in digital customer experience (no mobile app), data & analytics maturity, automated loan classification, and the absence of a card management system. Total estimated investment to close all gaps ranges from VND 150-250 billion (USD 6-10 million) IKIGAI ESTIMATE over 18-24 months.

1. Gaps vs Current Business Needs

These are capabilities required to operate the existing cash loan and installment loan business effectively.

1.1 Loan Origination

1.1.1
Gap: **End-to-end digital origination**
Current State: Manual/semi-automated via LOOMS (under migration); no customer self-service origination
Required State: Fully digital origination with customer-initiated applications via mobile/web; real-time decisioning; STP >60% ESTIMATE
Severity: **CRITICAL**
Impact: Customer experience; processing cost; competitive positioning
1.1.2
Gap: **Automated credit decisioning**
Current State: CIC-based scoring with limited GINI (26%); manual credit assessment for certain segments
Required State: AI/ML-powered decisioning engine with >20 models (AND Solution planned); real-time scoring; automated approval for low-risk segments
Severity: **HIGH**
Impact: Approval speed; risk accuracy; operational efficiency
1.1.3
Gap: **eKYC at scale**
Current State: FPT eKYC used during onboarding; VND 14B annual cost for eKYC + credit inquiry
Required State: Embedded eKYC within mobile app; biometric authentication for returning customers; reduced per-transaction cost
Severity: **HIGH**
Impact: Cost reduction; customer friction; compliance
1.1.4
Gap: **Document management**
Current State: LOOMS DMS V3.0 under implementation; currently fragmented
Required State: Centralized, searchable, compliant document management with automated classification and retention
Severity: **MEDIUM**
Impact: Audit readiness; operational efficiency
