Overview

This remediation plan addresses the findings identified in the ACF Operational Due Diligence Report v1.0 and Technology Due Diligence Report v1.0. Actions are prioritized based on severity, regulatory urgency, and business impact. Each action is assigned an owner, timeline, impact assessment, dependencies, and priority level (P1 = Must Do, P2 = Should Do, P3 = Nice to Have).

Key Dependencies and Risks

1. AFS Japan Capital & Alignment
Risk: Unable to fund transformation; continued losses
Mitigate: Regular Stecomi alignment; clear business case per initiative
2. T24 Vendor Responsiveness
Risk: Loan classification non-compliant; product delays
Mitigate: Escalate via AFS Group; explore R25 direct engagement
3. LOOMS (Newgen) Delivery
Risk: LOS migration delayed; manual processes continue
Mitigate: Weekly monitoring; DSD escalation; contingency plan
4. CMS Vendor Selection
Risk: Credit card launch delayed beyond 2027
Mitigate: Accelerate RFP decision (v04 completed)
5. Regulatory Environment
Risk: Changes to FinCo regulations, rate caps, restrictions
Mitigate: Regulatory monitoring; VNBA engagement; legal advisory
6. Portfolio Quality Stabilization
Risk: Losses exceed capital buffer; SBV intervention
Mitigate: Aggressive tightening (underway); collection investment
7. Talent Availability
Risk: Unable to hire risk modelers, data scientists, IT specialists in competitive Vietnam market
Mitigate: Competitive compensation; remote work; AEON brand leverage
