Executive Summary

This document establishes the Process KPI Framework for ABC, defining measurable performance indicators for each core process (Origination, Servicing, Collections, Customer Service, Compliance Reporting). Each KPI includes the current baseline (where available from Team 2 operational data), Year 1 target, Year 3 target, and industry benchmark. The framework covers 48 KPIs across 5 process domains, providing the measurement backbone for ABC's digital transformation. IKIGAI PROJECTION
