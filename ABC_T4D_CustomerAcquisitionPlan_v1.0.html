Executive Summary

ABC currently acquires customers through two channels: telesales (67%, dominated by ACS cross-sell) and NSN/POS dealers (33%). Digital acquisition is non-existent. This Customer Acquisition Plan establishes a multi-channel acquisition engine targeting 80,000 customers in Year 1, 150,000 in Year 2, and 300,000 in Year 3, with digital channels progressively becoming the dominant acquisition source. IKIGAI PROJECTION

The plan centers on five acquisition pillars: (1) Digital Marketing across Facebook, TikTok, Google, and Zalo; (2) KOL/Influencer partnerships; (3) Agent Banking optimization via VPBank NEO and ABC RetailiABC Vietnam Ecosystem — "Lotte vs AEON – Vietnam Ecosystem.pdf" OtherData cross-sell; (4) Referral Programs through WAONiABC Group Loyalty Program Documentation loyalty; and (5) Employer Partnership Programs. Total Year 1 acquisition budget is VND 12.1B (USD 484KESTIMATE), scaling to VND 21.3B (USD 852K) by Year 3.

1.5 Creative Strategy

Vietnamese-First Content Principles

**Language**
100% Vietnamese; no code-switching; colloquial tone matching target demographic
**Visual Style**
Bright, modern, clean design; ABC brand colors (purple/pink/white); Asian faces and settings
**Mobile Optimization**
All creative in 9:16 vertical format; auto-play video; captions always on (sound-off viewing)
**Trust Building**
SBV license badge; ABC Japan parent; customer testimonials; transparent rate display
**Call-to-Action**
Clear Vietnamese CTA: "Dang ky ngay" (Register now); "Tai app" (Download app); "Nhan uu dai" (Get offer)
**A/B Testing**
