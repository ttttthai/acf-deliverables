Executive Summary

This document provides text-based BPMN process descriptions for ABC's 5 core processes (Loan Origination, Loan Servicing, Collections, Customer Service, Compliance Reporting) in both current and future states. The analysis identifies 27 process bottlenecks, 19 automation opportunities ranked by ROI, 12 handoff inefficiencies, and 8 critical control gaps. The future-state processes target 80% straight-through processing (STP) for origination, 60% digital collection resolution, and fully automated SBV regulatory reporting. IKIGAI TARGET
