Executive Summary

This report provides detailed profiles of seven major competitors in Vietnam's consumer finance market. Each profile covers company overview, products and channels, financial performance, technology and digital capabilities, marketing strategy, and ecosystem positioning. A competitive scoring matrix rates each competitor across key dimensions on a 1-5 scale, providing ACF with a clear view of where it leads and lags relative to peers.

1.5 Marketing and Brand Strategy

  • Strong brand recognition as market leader
  • Digital marketing via social media, TikTok, and influencer partnerships
  • Focus on transparency messaging ("Transparent and Accessible Consumer Finance")
  • Aggressive 2WL campaigns promoting e-motorbike financing
  • Customer migration strategy from physical to FE ONLINE 2.0 platform

1.6 Competitive Assessment
