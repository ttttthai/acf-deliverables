Executive Summary

ACF's current sales and distribution model is dangerously concentrated: 67% of disbursements come from telesales (dominated by ACS cross-sell at 60% of total volume) and 33% from the National Sales Network (POS dealers), with less than 1% from digital channels. This concentration creates single-channel dependency risk, limits addressable market, and results in high customer acquisition costs (estimated VND 400-500K per customer via telesales vs. industry digital CAC of VND 50ESTIMATE-150K).

Meanwhile, competitors are racing ahead: FE Credit has 14M+ customers on FE ONLINE 2.0; Home Credit dominates with HomePayLater across 26,000+ MWG retail touchpoints; VietCredit's digital-first model achieved 89.4% growth with the lowest CIR in the sector (22.1%); and MCredit achieves 2-minute API-based loan approval through the MB Bank ecosystem.

This Sales & Distribution Strategy rebalances ACF's channel mix from telesales-dominant to a diversified, digitally-led model. The target is to shift from 67/33/<1 (telesales/NSN/digital) to 25/20/55IKIGAI PROJECTION (telesales/NSN+merchant/digital+partner) within 3 years, dramatically reducing CAC while expanding the addressable market from ~50K active accounts to 800K+ customers.

1.2 Performance by Channel

ACS Cross-Sell (Telesales) -- BEST PERFORMER

  • Volume: VND 61BIKIGAI PROJECTION in January 2026 (60% of total disbursements)
  • Approval Rate: 70.6% (+6.6% MoM improvement)
  • Average Ticket: VND 46.4M for cash loans (221% of budget)
  • NPL Quality: Best among all channels -- existing ACS installment customers with known payment behavior
  • CAC: Lowest (VND 200-300KESTIMATE) -- existing customer base requires no acquisition marketing
  • Assessment: ACF's strongest channel. The cross-sell from AEON installment customers (ACS) to cash loans demonstrates the ecosystem monetization potential. This channel should be aggressively scaled.
  • Limitation: Addressable base is limited to existing ACS installment members (~700KESTIMATE total; ~200K addressable for cross-sell)

NSN (National Sales Network / POS Dealers) -- UNDERPERFORMING

  • Volume: VND 33.8BESTIMATE (33% of total; declined vs December due to Tet and Cho Lon suspension)
  • Approval Rate: 73.6i"Loan_application_flow.xlsx" — Installment Approval Rate, Package 6 Mar 2026% for installment CD; 61.7% for installment TW
  • FPD Performance by Dealer: Highly variable
    • Cho Lon: 18.3% FPD5 -- SUSPENDED
    • MediaMart: 52.9% FPD -- SHOULD BE SUSPENDED
    • Multiple individual stores: 100% FPD5+ (very small samples)
  • Geographic Risk: Lai Chau 25.0% FPD5; Hanoi 15.9% FPD5; Hau Giang 11.1%
  • CAC: Highest (VND 800-1,200KESTIMATE) -- includes commission, co-location costs, and marketing support
  • Assessment: Channel requires significant cleanup. High-FPD dealers must be suspended. Quality dealers should be retained and optimized. New dealer acquisition should focus on AEON ecosystem and quality electronics retailers.

Digital Channel -- NON-EXISTENT

  • Volume: <1% of total
  • Current Capability: No customer-facing mobile app; basic Customer Portal (web); Sales App for internal use only
  • Competitive Gap: FE Credit (14M app users), Home Credit (full app), MCredit (2-min approval), VietCredit (digital-first with 89.4% growth)
  • Assessment: CRITICAL gap. Without a digital channel, ACF cannot compete for the growing segment of digitally-native borrowers, cannot reduce CAC, and cannot scale efficiently.

VPBank NEO (Agent Banking) -- NASCENT

  • Structure: ACF lending products distributed via VPBank NEO super app through agent banking model
  • Regulatory Status: Compliance framework being finalized (KYC responsibility, data sharing, consumer disclosure)
  • Volume: Small but growing
  • Assessment: High potential channel providing nationwide reach without ACF branch infrastructure. Requires optimization of application flow, ACF-branded interface, and data sharing agreements.

Agent Network Right-Sizing

Recommendations:

  • Reduce or exit: Underperforming agents in high-FPD provinces (Lai Chau, Hau Giang)
