Executive Summary

This migration plan defines the phased technology transformation for ACF across 4 phases over 60 months. Each phase includes detailed activities, dependencies, risks, resource requirements, success criteria, and rollback plans. The plan is designed to maintain business continuity while progressively modernizing ACF's technology estate from its current state (maturity 1.3/5) to the target architecture (maturity 4.4/5).

LOOMS Rollback Plan: IKIGAI DESIGN

  1. Trigger: Critical defect affecting >10% of applications OR system unavailability >4 hours
  2. Decision: CTO + Head of Development + Head of Operations
  3. Action: Redirect application intake to legacy NewGen LOS; LOOMS taken offline for remediation
  4. Data: All LOOMS applications during parallel period replicated to NewGen; no data loss
  5. Timeline: 8 hours for full rollback including communication to business users IKIGAI DESIGN
  6. Post-rollback: Root cause analysis within 48 hours; revised go-live plan within 2 weeks IKIGAI DESIGN
